Choice Of Components



CPU

The Intel® Core™ i7-13700K processor is a high-end CPU for gaming. It features 8 cores and 16 processing threads, with a base frequency of 3.8 GHz that can reach up to 5.0 GHz in turbo mode, providing great processing power and performance in high-demand tasks like video games.

CPU Cooler

The Be Quiet Silent Loop 2 360mm is an all-in-one liquid cooling solution designed for high-end processors. With a 360mm radiator and a water block optimized for maximum contact with the processor surface, the Silent Loop 2 provides efficient and quiet cooling even in high load situations.

Motherboard

The Gigabyte Z790 AORUS Master is a high-performance gaming motherboard that offers a wide range of features and customization options to satisfy the needs of the most demanding users. It features a Z790 chipset and supports 11th and 12th generation Intel processors, up to 128 GB of RAM, and a wide range of ports and connectors, including Thunderbolt 4.

Memory

The Kingston FURY Beast 64 GB (2 x 32 GB) 3200 MHz is a high-speed and high-capacity RAM kit designed for gaming and high-demand applications. With a frequency of 3200 MHz and low latency of CL16, this memory provides superior performance in high-demand tasks and multitasking.

Storage

The Crucial P3 1TB M.2 PCIe Gen3 is a high-speed solid-state drive designed for gaming and applications that require high read and write speeds. With a capacity of 1TB, a read speed of up to 3400 MB/s, and a write speed of up to 3000 MB/s, this SSD is ideal for games and applications that require quick access to data.

Graphics Card

The ASUS TUF Gaming NVIDIA GeForce RTX™ 4070 Ti is a high-end graphics card designed for gaming and high-demand graphic applications. With the NVIDIA Ampere architecture and 12 GB of GDDR6 memory, this graphics card provides superior performance in games and applications that require high graphic processing power.
